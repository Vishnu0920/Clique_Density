5. Conclusion

Although both algorithms target the exact densest subgraph, their methodologies and efficiencies differ:

Aspect Exact CoreExact
Search SpaceEntire GraphReduced to dense cores
Memory UsageHighMuch lower
PreprocessingNoneRequires core decomposition
ScalabilityLimited for large graphsEfficient even for bigger graphs

In summary, CoreExact offers a scalable refinement of the Exact method by intelligently narrowing the search space before applying expensive network flow computations.
